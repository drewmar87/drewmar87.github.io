**Instructions For Localized areas**
  1. Change the word 'Generalized' to 'Localized'.
  2. You can have more than one localized section.
  3. Add the specific teeth numbers and locations related to the descriptor of choice.
  4. Add the localized paragraph immediately following the generalized paragraph.
  5. Example: "Localized magenta margin located around facials of: #10, 11, 20, 21, 30 with localized exudate facial #10."
  6. Example 2: "Localized blunted papilla interproximal between 8-9, 9-10, and 24-25 with recessed margins facial #21, 24, 28, and 29."
